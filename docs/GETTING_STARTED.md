## Platform Connections

### Connecting Social Media Accounts

Before you can schedule and publish posts, you need to connect your social media accounts.

#### For Platform Owners (One-Time Setup)

If you're setting up the platform for your organization:

1. Go to **Admin** ‚Üí **OAuth Credentials**
2. For each platform you want to support:
   - Create an OAuth app on the platform's developer portal
   - Enter the Client ID and Client Secret
   - Set the redirect URI
   - Save the credentials (they're encrypted automatically)

#### For Users (One-Click Connection)

Once OAuth is configured, users can connect their accounts easily:

1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Platforms**
2. Find the platform you want to connect (Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, etc.)
3. Click **"Connect Platform"**
4. You'll be redirected to the platform's authorization page
5. Grant permissions
6. You'll be redirected back automatically
7. Your account is now connected!

### Supported Platforms

- **Twitter/X**: Tweet scheduling and publishing
- **LinkedIn**: Post and article publishing
- **Facebook**: Page post publishing
- **Instagram**: Photo and video posts
- **TikTok**: Video upload and publishing
- **YouTube**: Video upload and publishing

### Connection Status

- **Connected** ‚úÖ: Platform is connected and ready to use
- **Ready to Connect** üîµ: OAuth is configured, click to connect
- **Not Configured** ‚ö†Ô∏è: Platform owner needs to configure OAuth credentials

## Creating Your First Post

### Step 1: Open the Post Creator

1. Click **"Create New Post"** on the dashboard, or
2. Go to **Scheduler** ‚Üí Click **"Create Post"**

### Step 2: Write Your Content

1. Enter your post content in the text area
2. Use the character counter to stay within platform limits
3. Add hashtags and mentions as needed

### Step 3: Add Media (Optional)

1. Click **"Add Image"** or **"Add Link"**
2. Upload an image or enter a URL
3. Use the **Media Library** to reuse previously uploaded assets

### Step 4: Select Platform

1. Choose the platform(s) you want to publish to
2. The content will be validated for the selected platform(s)

### Step 5: Schedule Your Post

1. Choose a **date** and **time** for publishing
2. Or select **"Publish Now"** for immediate publishing

### Step 6: Preview & Validate

1. Click the **"Preview"** tab to see how your post will look
2. Check platform-specific validation:
   - Character limits
   - Hashtag format
   - Image size/format
   - Link preview

### Step 7: Save or Publish

1. Click **"Save as Draft"** to save for later, or
2. Click **"Schedule Post"** to schedule it, or
3. Click **"Publish Now"** to publish immediately

### Using AI Features

#### AI Content Generator

1. Click the **"AI"** tab in the post creator
2. Enter a topic or description
3. Click **"Generate Content"**
4. Review and edit the generated content
5. Use it in your post

#### AI Post Optimizer

1. Write your content
2. Click **"Optimize with AI"**
3. Review optimization suggestions
4. Apply changes

### Using Templates

1. Click the **"Templates"** tab
2. Browse available templates
3. Click **"Use Template"** to load a template
4. Customize the content
5. Save as a new post

## Troubleshooting

### Posts Not Publishing

1. Check platform connection status
2. Verify OAuth permissions
3. Check post validation errors
4. Review error messages in post details

### Connection Issues

1. Verify OAuth credentials are configured (admin)
2. Try disconnecting and reconnecting
3. Check platform API status
4. Contact support if issues persist

### Import/Export Issues

1. Verify file format (CSV/JSON)
2. Check data format matches requirements
3. Review error messages
4. Try exporting a sample first
